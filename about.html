My Journey with Content Creation

All my life, my print handwriting has been described with a plethora of adjectives from "pretty" to "perfect." I begain learning calligraphy when the world shut down in March 2020 and received many compliments on my work. In December of that same year, I thought it would be fun to dedicate a TikTok account to my handwriting and handlettering. Little did I know: This would become my business.

TikTok is a fascinating platform in that it's possible no one may see your video, or millions of people could view within a few hours. One day, I had 200 followers; the next morning, I had 22,700. From that moment, I began catering my content towards my audience's requests and needs. More importantly, over time, I've observed patterns that have helped me understand the TikTok algorithm. As a result, several of my videos have "gone viral," including a few of my sponsored posts. I received my first request for a brand collaboration in February 2020, and since then, I've worked with Pentel, Speechify, Fonts App, Gauthmath, StudySmarter, and more. My sponsored video for StudySmarter (seen to the right) currently has over 3.7 million views and 285,000 likes.
