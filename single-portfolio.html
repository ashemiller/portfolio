TikTok Ad for Fonts App

  • Download
  • July 2021
  • Technology Used: Phone camera, VN app
  • Category: Digital Marketing

Situation & Task

Fonts App is a mobile app that allows users to download different fonts to their smartphone keyboard. The Fonts App social media marketing team reached out to me and asked that I create an organic ad incorporating my handwriting with the goal of persuading viewers to download Fonts App. Their team found me through my TikTok page @mywritingaesthetic where over 670,000 people follow me for handwriting tutorials, study tips, and overall aesthetic writing. Fonts App expressed interest in working with me as a creator because they thought that incorporating my handwriting into a video could be unique. Thus, in this situation, Fonts App asked that I create content for their verified brand TikTok page rather than to promote on my own page.

Actions

To make this video, I used my brother’s Razer phone to film my handwriting in 4k/60fps. I then edited the clips in the VN app to give the illusion that my font imitation writing transformed into the Fonts App brand font. I uploaded the edited video draft to Dropbox, where I shared the link with the Fonts App team for review.

Results

This project was a good challenge because my previous experience with branded content had been for sponsored videos on my TikTok page, so this was my first time creating content for another brand’s audience. Fonts App loved the video and asked me to create a second one. As of October 2021, my videos posted on the Fonts App TikTok page had been viewed over 26,000 times with approximately 2,000 likes.
